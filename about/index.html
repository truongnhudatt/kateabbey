  • Photographer/Director:

    I’ve won awards, exhibited work, created two awesome kids (with the help of Ian), realised I’m addicted to sports that involve break-neck speeds, secured representation in London, New York and Shanghai and fostered an utter, unquenchable thirst for capturing those intimate moments that make us feel alive.

    Trusted by the biggest names in the industry and equally happy shooting stills or moving image, I totally get under the skin of each brief in my grasp. I try and take everything I do that extra mile, wrestling with my images to pin down the ultimate perfection, but all with the aim of appearing completely effortless.

    I do all this with the deep collaboration of my amazing crew, who always make me laugh.
